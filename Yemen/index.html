A Timeline of US Troops in Yemen

The amount of troops deployed in Yemen has fluctuated significantly over the past decade as strategic pursuits have changed in the Middle East.

By Alex Percey

Yemen has been a strategic location for US military operations as they attempted to stop the Houthis and support Saudi efforts to reclaim their country. This was most active during the late Obama administration in the mid-2010's, and the amount of troops in the area has greatly decreased since then. As of now, very few troops occupy the area.
